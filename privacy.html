Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. kragerlabs.com uses cookies to help kragerlabs.com identify and track visitors, their usage of kragerlabs.com website, and their website access preferences. kragerlabs.com visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using kragerlabs.com's websites, with the drawback that certain features of kragerlabs.com's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
